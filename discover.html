Discover UMD

Insights about UMD and Duluth
Video Credit: Addison Manley, Danica Silcox, Lauren Sisco

Why I chose UMD

I chose to attend the University of Minnesota Duluth because I was drawn to its welcoming and well-designed campus. I appreciate that the entire campus is connected, which is especially convenient during the colder months. Additionally, UMD’s size offers the ideal balance—not too large and not too small—allowing for a strong sense of community while still providing ample opportunities. I was also impressed by the university’s highly regarded business school, which I believe will support my academic and professional success.

What I like about Duluth and the Surrounding Area

Duluth is a beautiful city with many remarkable sights and activities to enjoy. I especially appreciate being close to Lake Superior and having the opportunity to take in its stunning views throughout the city. The surrounding areas also offer plenty to explore, including scenic state parks such as Jay Cooke and local favorites like Betty’s Pies. The Duluth community is welcoming, and the people here are kind and supportive.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

Some of my favorite places on campus include the Kathryn A. Martin Library and the Kirby Student Center. The library provides a wide range of resources to support students academically and offers an excellent environment for studying. The Kirby Student Center is another favorite spot—it’s a welcoming place to connect with friends while also providing a comfortable atmosphere to focus on coursework. A personal experience I value deeply is the academic growth I’ve experienced at the Labovitz School of Business and Economics. I initially entered UMD as a marketing major, but through the guidance and support of my professors and advisors, I discovered that accounting better aligns with my interests and long-term goals.
