Personal Interests

My Hobbies

Activities that enrich my life and personal growth

Tennis

Tennis has been a cornerstone of my personal development and family bonding. I grew up playing this sport and it quickly became one of my favorite activities. I joined my high school's varsity team, where I experienced both the competitive thrill and the invaluable social aspects of team sports. Some of my closest friendships were forged through being teammates, creating lasting bonds that extend far beyond the court.

What makes tennis particularly special to me is its role in strengthening family relationships. My father served as my coach, and my sister was also on the team, making it a true family affair. This shared passion has been instrumental in building the close relationship I have with my father, and I'm deeply grateful for the opportunity to share this love of the sport with my family.

Reading

Reading provides me with entertainment that feels both engaging and intellectually productive, offering something that television simply cannot match. While I enjoy exploring various literary genres, mystery and thriller novels hold a special place in my heart. I've discovered that stepping outside my comfort zone with different book genres can lead to wonderful surprises, and some of my highest-rated books have been found through this adventurous approach.

My passion for reading began in middle school when my sister and I would read the same books and discuss them together, creating meaningful conversations and shared experiences. This hobby naturally led me to apply for a position at my local library, and that experience has continued throughout my college years as I now work in the library on my college campus, combining my love for books with professional development.

Hiking

Hiking offers me the perfect opportunity to connect with nature and find peace in the great outdoors. Whenever I have the chance to experience beautiful weather on a scenic trail, I always embrace it wholeheartedly. Being surrounded by nature brings me genuine happiness and a deep sense of relaxation that's hard to find elsewhere.

This activity has become a cherished family tradition, as we all share a love for hiking and make it a priority during our travels. One of my most memorable hiking experiences was on the Silver Creek Trail at Jay Cooke State Park, which left a lasting impression on me. Hiking will always hold a special place in my life as a reliable stress-reliever and a healthy activity I can continue to enjoy throughout my lifetime.
